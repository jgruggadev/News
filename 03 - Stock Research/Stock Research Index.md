## Note Template

Each company note lives at `03 - Stock Research/[TICKER] - [Company Name].md` and follows this structure:

```
# [TICKER] — [Company Name]
Rating: [Outperform / Neutral / Underperform / Watchlist]
Price Target: $[XX.XX]
Current Price: $[XX.XX] as of [date]
Implied Upside: [X]%

## Business Overview
[2-3 sentences: what the company does, how it makes money, why it matters]

## Investment Thesis
[3 bullet points: the non-obvious reasons to own or avoid this stock]

## Variant Perception
[Where is consensus wrong? What is the market missing or over-pricing?]

## Key Metrics to Watch
[Revenue growth, margins, FCF yield, specific KPIs for this business]

## Valuation
[EV/EBITDA, P/E, DCF summary — vs. peers and vs. history]

## Catalysts
[Specific upcoming events that could prove the thesis right or wrong]

## Risks
[What could make this thesis wrong? Be specific and falsifiable]

## What Would Change My Mind
[Precise conditions that would flip the rating]

## Earnings Notes
[Running log of earnings call takeaways]
```
